From a preliminary check, it seems ORB is more reliable for key point tracking based off the imagery of checkered boards and small background objects which will be relevant in the test grounds used for the VO pipeline (camera being moved around my room or library with checkered pattern in frame at the start or end). This is qualitatively evident in ORB_test1 versus SIFT_test1 when comparing how many keypoints were assigned to the checkboard versus a fairly small background object and how many points were accurately tracked across orientation. SIFT_test1 assigns way more key points around the smaller background object and way less to what would be desirable to be a main focus, the checkered pattern, while still fairly inaccurately casting the points across it.
